# MaTTeRPixel Network - Design System & Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Identity & Strategy
**Mission**: To provide free modded servers and cultivate a welcoming, friendly community where players can escape daily stress and immerse themselves in the expansive sandbox world of Minecraft.
**Secret Sauce**: Best-in-class hardware for completely lag-free servers, managed and configured by the best minds in modded Minecraft.
**Keywords**: Welcoming, Community-focused, Innovative, Gaming, Sandbox, Premium, Lag-Free, Expert.

## 2. Target Audience
* **Core Demographic**: Mature players who appreciate a well-managed, lag-free environment.
* **Psychographics**: Players looking for a "home" server, people who value a safe, friendly, and mature environment, and users seeking long-term modding experiences without performance hiccups.
* **Needs**: Clear server status, easy-to-find connection info, stable and lag-free servers.

## 3. Tone of Voice
* **Friendly & Inviting**: We use language that makes every player feel at home, but caters to a mature playerbase.
* **Passionate & Enthusiastic**: We share the players' love for gaming and Minecraft.
* **Clear & Supportive**: When explaining rules, modpack installations, or technical details, we are concise and helpful.

## 4. Visual Design Rules
Based on the provided logos, our visual identity marries the blocky, voxel aesthetic of Minecraft with ultra-modern, premium web design.

### Color Palette
* **Primary**: `hsl(0, 100%, 50%)` (MaTTeR Red) - Used for primary actions, highlights, and emphasis.
* **Secondary**: `hsl(0, 0%, 80%)` (Pixel Silver) - Used for secondary elements, borders, and subtle text.
* **Background (Dark Mode First)**: 
  * Base: `hsl(240, 10%, 4%)` (Deep Void)
  * Surface: `hsl(240, 10%, 8%)` (Obsidian)
* **Text**: 
  * High Contrast: `hsl(0, 0%, 100%)` (Pure White)
  * Muted: `hsl(240, 5%, 65%)` (Stone Grey)

### Typography
* **Headings**: A blocky, bold font (e.g., 'Minecraftia' or a modern heavy sans like 'Outfit' with tight letter spacing) to reflect the logo.
* **Body**: 'Inter' or 'Roboto' for high legibility and a clean, premium feel.

### Aesthetics & Micro-interactions
* **Glassmorphism**: Use frosted glass effects (`backdrop-filter: blur()`) on navigation bars and floating cards to give a sleek, modern Boxxy-like feel.
* **Hover States**: Interactive elements should feature smooth, vibrant red glows (`box-shadow`) and slight upward translations to feel responsive and alive.
* **Borders**: Subtle `1px` borders with low opacity white (`rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)`) to define geometry without cluttering the screen.
